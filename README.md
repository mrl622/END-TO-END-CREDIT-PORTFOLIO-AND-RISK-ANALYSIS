# END-TO-END-CREDIT-PORTFOLIO-AND-RISK-ANALYSIS
📌 Project Overview
This end-to-end analytics project evaluates a ₹4.01 Billion loan portfolio to identify growth opportunities and mitigate financial risk. By integrating SQL querying, Excel ETL, and Power BI visualization, I transformed raw loan data into a strategic roadmap for portfolio recovery and regional expansion.
🛠️ Tech Stack
SQL (Databricks/Spark SQL): Data aggregation, joining relational tables, and filtering high-value defaults.
Power BI: Interactive dashboarding, KPI tracking, and regional waterfall analysis.
Excel (Power Query): Data cleaning, transformation, and "GST 2.0 Decoding" logic.
📊 Key Data Insights
Revenue Concentration: 70% of annual income is generated from top-tier cities (Chennai & Bhopal).
Risk Profile: The portfolio maintains an average CIBIL score of 696 (Fair range), requiring cautious high-value lending.
Payment Efficiency: UPI is the dominant transaction method, indicating a shift toward digital-first recovery.
Regional Growth: The North and South regions are the strongest contributors to portfolio increase.
🚀 Strategic Recommendations
Targeted Growth: Launch a "Premium Loan Product" specifically for high-income earners in Chennai and Bhopal.
Digital Recovery: Implement an automated WhatsApp/UPI reminder system to lower collection costs.
Risk Audit: Perform a deeper audit on North region applicants to understand and replicate their high growth contribution.
